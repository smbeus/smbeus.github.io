Hi! I'm Saga

Studio Beus is where I share all my creative work. I have a BA in Studio Art and an MA in Museum Studies, so my life basically revolves around making, studying, and talking about art! I design and create colorful pieces inspired by childhood memories and landscapes. I also post personal essays and other pieces of writing under the "Writing" tab.

Everything here was made in my home "studio" (aka a corner of our apartment) in Brooklyn. Prints are all hand-carved and printed by me in limited edition runs. 3D prints are designed and printed in-house by me and my partner Matt.

Thanks for taking a look around!

- Saga Beus
